SENSORY DINING EXPERIENCES


JAM's is a progression of rare and beautiful ingredients where texture, flavour and harmony is paramount. Continually inspired by nature, the creative process for chef Peter Gilmore begins in working closely with the farmers, fishermen, producers and artisans who cultivate bespoke produce exclusively for JAM. For each dish, the growth of every element and the selection of every ceramic piece is carefully considered and crafted for its role in the dining experience. JAM's brings a personal, interactive passage for diners through Peter Gilmore’s culinary evolution.
